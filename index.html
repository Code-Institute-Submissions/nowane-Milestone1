About Us

ze Gym is an ultra-modern fitness center with excellent amenities and training equipment. We offer a wide range of classes and exercising programs, tailored to improve stamina and fitness goals of each gym member.

We aim to create a system in which everyone is respected and to provide a unique and enjoyable environment in which everyone feels comfortable. ze Gym strives to enable everyone to be the best version of themselves. From the staff to the clients involved in the gym it is all about working for the greater good. Members at ze Gym can each select a trainer, training program and a membership option by choice and personal preferences.

We hope to see you soon!

Classes

HIIT

Every day we make great efforts at juggling demands of different sorts. High intensity interval training, or HIIT Workout, is a great way to fit in a rigorous workout in a short time span and make it fun to boot! It can deliver as good or better fitness benefits than steady state cardio (SSC), such as running, but in less time per day and week. A HIIT workout is the best fitness value for your time.

Bodypump

BODYPUMP™ is a barbell workout for anyone looking to get lean, toned and fit – fast. Using light to moderate weights with lots of repetition, BODYPUMP is a total body workout that burns up to 540 calories*. With scientifically proven moves and great, pumping music, it’s a fantastic and motivating class for instructors to teach. Are you ready to feel the burn?

Boxing

Boxing is a cardio workout in which multiple elements of training are combined. During this lesson, we will make use of punching bags and boxing gloves. Multiple hit and punch combinations will be treated during this lesson. During this group lesson, you will work individually or in pairs to train your condition. endurance, and muscle strength. Are you ready to sweat and burn calories?

Spinning

A challenging, stimulating and fun training: spinning is all of this and more. You are challenged to extend your boundaries, to go much further than you thought you could. It is a training for everyone, as you can determine the intensity by adjusting the resistance of your bike. Musicwill guide you through this hour, with fun on top of our priority list. It is recommended to bring a towel and a water bottle.

Pilates

Pilates is an innovative and safe way of movement and provides exercises for body and mind. Pilates was born from the movement principles of Joseph Pilates and can transform the way your body looks and functions. The goal of Pilates is not only to move but also to strengthen the connection between body and mind and therefore Pilates will not only make you feel fitter but also mentally stronger.

Yoga

It’s time to roll out your yoga mat and discover the combination of physical and mental exercises that for thousands of years have hooked yoga practitioners around the globe. Whether you are young or old, overweight or fit, yoga has the power to calm the mind and strengthen the body. Don’t be intimidated by yoga terminology, fancy yoga studios and complicated poses. Yoga is for everyone.
